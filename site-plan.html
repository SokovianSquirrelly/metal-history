Heavy metal is arguably one of the most complex network of music genres that we have and has a long history of evolving music and impacting culture...

Images for the Home page

Early Rock 'n Roll

Our journey starts in the late '40s throughout the '50s with rock n' roll. Much of early rock n' roll was inspired by African American music such as jazz, blues, and gospel. Typically, rock n' roll music had a dance-like rhythm with a strong backbeat...

Images for Early Rock n' Roll

British Invasion of '60s Rock

In the 1960s, rock bands from the United Kingdom started gaining traction with their takes on rock n' roll. The sound was heavier and more raw than the rock n' roll music in America...

Images for British Invasion of '60s Rock

The Birth of Heavy Metal

In 1970, a band from Birmingham, England called Black Sabbath released their debut album. Their music was darker and heavier than the raw sound of the '60s hard rock bands and it started a new genre that we know today as heavy metal...

Images for The Birth of Heavy Metal

Origins of Punk

While not technically considered part of the metal family, punk rock evolved alongside metal and even became intertwined into other metal subgenres. Punk was the genre of choice for people who wanted to rebel against the social norm...
