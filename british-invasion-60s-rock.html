British Invasion of '60s Rock

In the 1960s, rock bands from the United Kingdom started gaining traction with their takes on rock n' roll. The sound was heavier and more raw than the rock n' roll music in America. Much of this music was considered "hard rock" or just simply "rock".

Notable bands from this era include Led Zeppelin, The Rolling Stones, The Beatles, and The Doors.
