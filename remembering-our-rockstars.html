Remembering Our Rockstars

Throughout the decades, many notable people in the music scene have made widespread societal impacts, and many of those people are unfortunately no longer with us. These are some of those people who have passed on but we still honor their legacy.

Just as a disclaimer, there are some controversial figures on this list. While many people in this article are remembered fondly, there are a few that are not quite as well beloved due to their views or actions. The reason they may be on this list is because of either their influence or their high-profile deaths.
