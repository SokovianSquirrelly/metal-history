Welcome to Metal History!

Heavy metal is arguably one of the most complex network of music genres that we have and has a long history of evolving music and impacting culture. It has its roots in rock n' roll that started in the mid to late-1940s and was finally given to the world in 1970. Through history, heavy metal has evolved from that early inception in the early-70s in England, to the boom of thrash bands in the '80s, to the controversial rise of black metal in the early-90s, to the modern world where it is today.
